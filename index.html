Research Gap

1.Resource Scarcity?

We've observed a significant scarcity of resources dedicated to research and development in the domains of Sinhala and Tamil languages.There has been limited scholarly attention dedicated to these languages, presenting an opportunity for further exploration and advancement in linguistic studies.

2.Informal to Formal Translators

At present, there is a notable scarcity of informal to formal translators for Sinhala and Tamil languages. This limited availability poses a challenge for individuals seeking accurate translation services in various contexts. Addressing this gap could greatly enhance accessibility and communication for speakers of these languages .

3.Word Sense Disambiguation Problem

Currently, there are very few approaches to word sense disambiguation in Sinhala and Tamil languages, and the accuracy of existing methods is relatively low. This indicates a significant area for improvement and innovation in natural language processing for these languages, with potential benefits for various applications and users.

Methodology

The fundamental goal of this research is to develop a system that's capable of translating informal sentences into formal equivalents while also incorporating word sense disambiguation. Furthermore, the system has the goal of fixing grammatical faults in output formal sentences. Users can also acquire an English translation of the submitted informal sentence. The system consists of two main components for both Sinhala and Tamil languages.

  • Word sense disambiguation model
  • Informal to formal translator model The following image depicts high level architecture of the implemented system.

Proposed Problem

Current translation systems predominantly focus on formal language, overlooking the nuances and complexities of informal language. This creates a barrier to effective communication in informal settings, hindering language access and comprehension. Addressing this gap requires advancements in informal language processing, particularly in areas such as word sense disambiguation, where the lack of research and difficulty in handling both languages pose significant challenges.

Proposed solution

his research addresses the challenge of translating informal Sinhala and Tamil sentences into their formal equivalents specifically tailored for grade five students. It recognizes the necessity of converting informal language forms into Standard Written Language Text (SWT) before undertaking computational tasks. The conversion of informal language into SWT is identified as a significant difficulty in the natural language processing (NLP). Both the languages are recognized as having less resources and high complexity which make hurdles in developing efficient translation systems. The principal aim of this study is to devise a robust translator that not only converts informal sentences to formal sentences but also resolves word sense disambiguation for both nouns and verbs. To enhance the user experience, the system offers the ability to get the grammatically corrected formal translation and the English translation of the initial input. To handle word sense ambiguity and the formal translation, a rule-based systemis used to ensure accuracy.

Research Objectives

1.Words Sense Disambiguation -Sinhala

Develop a machine learning model to identify a given informal Sinhala sentence along with resolving word sense disambiguation which will accurately analyse sentence structure and determines sentence type, while disambiguating words for clearer interpretation.​

2. ⁠Informal to formal translation -Sinhala

Utilize machine learning algorithms to translate a given informal Sinhala sentence into grammatically correct formal sentences which will analyse the sentence structure and applies appropriate grammar rules to ensure accurate translation.

3. Words Sense Disambiguation -Tamil

Develop a machine learning model to identify a given informal Tamil sentence along with resolving word sense disambiguation which will accurately analyse sentence structure and determines sentence type, while disambiguating words for clearer interpretation.​

4.Informal to formal translation -Tamil

Utilize machine learning algorithms to translate a given informal Tamil sentence into grammatically correct formal sentences which will analyse the sentence structure and applies appropriate grammar rules to ensure accurate translation.​
